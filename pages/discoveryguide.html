DISCOVERY GUIDE

The Discovery Guide is the City of Omaha Parks & Recreation's yearly publication. It is filled with all the programs and events that the department has to offer, as well as information about Omaha parks, golf, and trails. For this project, I worked with the existing Parks brand font and colors to create a cohesive magazine. Because there was a lot of information to include, the goal was to create a simple enough design to make it easy to read, but also be visually interesting.
